App Name: Hadeda Hunter

Description:
Hadeda Hunter is an all in one platform, created to benifit any adventurous person
in their ventures. This application can be used to log your observations, collaberate
 with other bird watchers, and ensure that you go out watching, knowing that the
conditions are optimal. Features that make it stand out include:
- Weather tile that provides information on the forcast for the day
- Bird watching conditions that are calculated based off the perfect weather conditions
- Observation logging
- Hotspot viewing 
- Hotspot saving
- Personal preferences(Hotspot radius)
- Catalogue of birds
These features alone prove that this app will assist you and improve your bird watching
experience.
